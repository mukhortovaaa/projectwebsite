My tennis career started when I was 7 years old and never have stopped. I was playing mostly tournamennts in Russia and did not have an opportunity to travel outside of Russia. Luckily, I went to Greece where I was noticed by a coach from Arizona State University. That tournament helped me to get into college tennis team that leaded to a fact that I transferred after just one semester to University of Wisconsin, Madison, where nowadays I am playing for the team and very happy to be.

Last year, I started from the third position in singles and was moved to second one. Overall, in 2021-22 season I finished with 17 wins, 7 losses, and 2 unfinished matches. In doubles all the year I played at number one position and has the statistic of 16 wins, 8 losses, and 1 unfinished match.
